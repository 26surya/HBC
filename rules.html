📜 HBC Game Rules

Welcome to Hum Banayenge Crorepati (HBC)! Here are the basic rules of the game:

  1. The game consists of 16 multiple-choice questions.
  2. Time Limits:
    • ⏳ Questions 1 to 5: You have 
       45 seconds
      per question.
    • ⏳ Questions 6 to 10: You have 
       60 seconds
      per question.
    • ⏳ Questions 11 to 16: No time limit! Take your time.
  3. Each correct answer moves you to the next level with a higher prize.
  4. You have four ONE lifeline to help you:
    • 📞 Phone a Friend - Call a friend for help.
    • 🎲 50-50 - Removes two incorrect answers.
    • 👥 Ask the Audience - Get audience poll results.
    • 🔄 Flip the Question - Get a new question.
      •
    • ✨ Jaadu - Blink the Correct Option.
  5. If you answer incorrectly, you will be eliminated instantly.
  6. You can choose to walk away with your 7 Crore at any time.
